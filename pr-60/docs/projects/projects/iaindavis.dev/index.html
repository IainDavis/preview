IainDavis.dev (Personal Website)

Home of IainDavis on the Web‚Äã

When I was included in a round of layoffs that closed our entire Sydney office, I thought I'd better start cultivating an internet presence that demonstrates how I work and think about software, but I didn't have any central place to share my work out (or any non-proprietary work I could share, for that matter). This project is the website I am building to resolve that deficit. It's here to serve as a place to collect demonstrative examples of my work, and to serve as one itself. It's also a central point of access to find me in various social media locations around the internet.
